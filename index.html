About

Myself

I'm a recent graduate from the University of York. As much as I enjoyed my degree in History of Art I felt an affinty towards coding and utilising my creative background. I created this website to showcase my abilities in Web & Graphic Design as well as Illustration.

I hold no formal qualifications in developing or graphic design, instead I rely on self-taught knowledge. This has stemmed from years of skateboarding, guitar, climbing, art and video games. I have a very self-driven attitude towards learning.
